The rules

  • The players take turns to be the actor (play passes to the left).
  • Choose the first actor (perhaps by playing the Random Country Game!).
  • Actor selects a random word using this website. If a word is presented which has already been used, click the button to get another word.
  • Actor has 30 seconds to prepare, and then:
    • Reads aloud the Clue (category)
    • Acts out the FIRST syllable
    • Acts out the SECOND syllable
    • Acts out the WHOLE WORD
  • After all the acting is complete, the remaining players can then guess what the full word is.
  • If someone guesses correctly, the actor congratulates them, and both the actor AND the guessor receive five points.
  • If no-one guesses correctly at first, the actor is allowed to re-enact all three mimes.
  • Notes:
    • The actor is allowed to pass and select a new word, but must do this before begining the mime. Every skipped word costs one point.
    • The actor is expected to act in silence, but can make non-verbal sounds (such as barking to imply a dog), as long as this is not close to the syllable being acted out.
    • Some degree of artistic license is allowed in pronunciation (such as repetition of consonants eg Chick Ken for chicken).
